About us

History Cakes etc.

Cakes Etc. originally is from Kelantan, Malaysia, now baking to serve at entire Malaysia. Cakes Etc. is an ongoing bakery organization specializing in developing quality bakery experiences throughout all Region of Malaysia with additional diversified businesses associated with the industry. Founded and driven by company entrepreneur, Mrs. Julia Bt Ramli. The company was established in 2003, at Kota Bharu, Kelantan, and now operates more than 20 high driven quality bakery chains with exciting projects under development, in addition to the portfolio-associated businesses within the portfolio. Using a family recipes passed down through the generations, the Cakes Etc. was an immediate success! Cakes Etc. owns and operates our chains by partnering with international pastry/bakery chefs to facilitate the development of our brands in the all-Region of Malaysia as we have built a strong platform in this industry. As a well-known brand in that region, we are aiming to expand our brand more so everyone in Malaysia and outside Malaysia can experience Cakes Etc. Cakes Etc. is also surrounded by a growing staff of great people who share her vision for creating the finest quality baked goods. Julia and her workers are enjoying their craft more than ever and look forward to serving you fresh and delicious cakes and pastries in the near future.
